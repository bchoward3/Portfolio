Manifest Destiny 2025: The New Westward Expansion

I wanted to examine a potential new demographic trend following the rhetoric of the 2024 presidential election. Donald Trump campagined on alleviating the overpriced housing market by selling larges swathes of land owned by the federal government. Using data from the Congressional Research Service I made a chloropleth map detailing the percentage of acreage owned by the federal government by state to see what states would be most affected.
