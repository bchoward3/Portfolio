Chatham County GA hurricane damage dashboard.

An interactive project.

Project Overview

This was a project for my interactive cartography graduate class and I decided to look at the impact of the last three major hurricane/tropical storms that affected my home of Chatham County, GA. The project used a combination of D3 for the Chatham County SVG map and Leaflet for the interactive storm tracker with a bar graph below to dipslay the damage data non-spatially.
